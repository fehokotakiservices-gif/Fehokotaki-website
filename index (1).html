Proven in Practice

From pilot to impact.

Before formally launching, Fehokotaki trialled its engagement services with Kāinga Ora residents in the Orākei community — demonstrating the power of culturally responsive, low-barrier engagement.

📷

Add a photo from your pilot events

November 2025 · Orākei

Community Bingo Event

  • 15 local residents attended and actively participated
  • Strengthened relationships between organisations and residents
  • Increased social cohesion and connection among neighbours
  • Demonstrated effectiveness of interactive, low-barrier engagement

December 2025 · Orākei

Community Christmas Lunch

  • 15–20 residents came together over a shared Christmas meal
  • Interactive games facilitated a positive, festive atmosphere
  • Improved social connection and neighbourhood networks
  • Built trust and repeat engagement with Kāinga Ora residents

Who We Serve

Partners in community-led change.

We work across the public and community sectors with organisations wanting to engage authentically with Pasifika and culturally diverse communities.

Local Government & Councils

Consultation, placemaking, and engagement strategies that meet legislative requirements and build genuine community trust.

Central Government Agencies

Place-based, social, and wellbeing initiatives requiring culturally competent engagement and community development expertise.

Iwi & Māori Organisations

Collaborative partnership with iwi and hapū, drawing on shared values of community wellbeing and long-term relationships.

Community Organisations & NGOs

Engagement support and programme co-design for organisations working with Pasifika, tamariki, whānau, and diverse communities.

Pasifika Communities

Lived experience and cultural grounding ensures our work is trusted, relevant, and meaningful for Pasifika people across Aotearoa.

Grassroots & Neighbourhood Groups

Supporting neighbourhood-led initiatives, social cohesion projects, and community capability-building at the local level.
